Internal Cybersecurity Hackathon

Overview

We are excited to announce that our Department of Cybersecurity is hosting an internal Cybersecurity Hackathon—a dynamic, multi-day event. This isn’t a typical 24-hour hackathon; you will have ample time throughout the days to refine your project and showcase your innovation.

Event Themes Include:

  • Digital Forensics & Cybercrime Investigation
  • Law Enforcement Cybersecurity Awareness & Training
  • Smart Policing & Digital Evidence Management
  • Child Safety & Online Predators Monitoring
  • Ransomware & Cyber Extortion Response
  • AI-Powered Fraud & Scam Detection
  • Cyber Threat Intelligence & Dark Web Monitoring
  • Open Category: Propose innovative solutions that directly enhance police operations and cybersecurity measures.

Team Structure

  • Team Size: 2 to 5 members

Hackathon Planning Timeline

  • 03/03/2025: Submissions Start
  • 05/03/2025: Submissions End
  • 07/03/2025: Round 1 Qualified Announced (online mails will be sent)
  • 08/03/2025: Round 2 Final Showdown – Winners Announced (offline; expert from TSPA will be the jury)

How to Participate

  1. Register by filling out the provided Google Form.
  2. Select your preferred theme. (If you choose the Open Category, ensure your proposal clearly demonstrates how it supports police and cybersecurity initiatives.)
  3. Submit a brief project overview along with any supporting documents.
